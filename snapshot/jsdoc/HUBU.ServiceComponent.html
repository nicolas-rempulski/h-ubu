Class: ServiceComponent

HUBU. ServiceComponent

This class represents _Service Components_. Service Components are one of the main concept of h-ubu. It represents the published and required services for a specific h-ubu's component. The constructor. Initializes the service component. By default, there are no service dependencies and no provided services.
